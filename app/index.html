What it does

  • Unlock — Remove open-password protection when you already know the password.
  • Lock — Add a password to PDFs you own before sharing.
  • Library — Keep files handy (Premium).

Processing stays on your device. The app is not for cracking unknown passwords — only for files you are allowed to modify.
